Sathyabama Coding Club

Coding Club of Sathyabama Institute of science and technology is administered by the Placement Cell. It aims to bring together the core talent of the Institute under one umbrella. The main vision of the club is to improve the art of competitive programming skills in the students and to make them ready for renowned competitions like ACM-ICPC, Google code jam, various Hackathons conducted at national and International levels etc. Team Work will remain the key of any successful club and thus the students are split into teams and are encouraged to work in teams. We develop positivity in the community with beautiful community standards.
